Mean Parameter (μ) Mean of the log-transformed variable ln(X). Controls the median of the log-normal distribution.
μ ∈ (-∞, ∞), where median = e^μ
μ=0: median=1, μ=1: median≈2.72, μ=-1: median≈0.37
Standard Deviation Parameter (σ) Standard deviation of ln(X). Controls spread and skewness of the log-normal distribution.
σ > 0, higher σ = more right-skewed and variable
σ=0.5: moderate skew, σ=1: pronounced skew, σ=2: heavy skew
